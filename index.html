Biography

I'm a Ph.D. candidate in the Data Engineering and Security Laboratory, School of Computer Science and Technology, Xidian University. My advisors are Prof. Cui Jiangtao and Prof. LI Hui. I got my B.Eng. degree from School of Computer Science and Technology in Xidian University in 2013. At the same year, I began my study in the successive postgraduate and doctoral programs. I've had the good fortune of working with some nice and hard-working scholars, including HE Bing, Hou Yongchao, Liu Yingfan, Peng Yanguo, Wang Manlin, WANG Meng, Ying Zuobin, Zhang Xiaoyu, Zhu Yixiao, etc.

I am interested in fundamental algorithms for large-scale data management applications (such as multimedia retrieval, unstructured database, high performance querying, etc). Now, I mainly focus on research of high-dimensional indexing and approximate query processings, including k nearest neighbor (kNN) search, k furthest neighbor (kFN) search, etc. I have got 2 academic papers published and 5 invention patents (to be authorized). Though in the initial stage, I maintain a keen curiosity in exploring enchanted algorithms/applications and a strong passion for technical practice. Now I am carrying on seeking for broader and deeper comprehension on my topics.
