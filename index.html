Biography

I'm a Ph.D. candidate in the Data Engineering and Security Laboratory, School of Computer Science and Technology, Xidian University. My advisors are Prof. Cui Jiangtao, Prof. LI Hui and Prof. Wei Wang. I got my B.Eng. degree from School of Computer Science and Technology in Xidian University in 2013. At the same year, I began my study in the successive postgraduate and doctoral programs. I've had the good fortune of working with some nice and hard-working scholars, including HE Bing, Hou Yongchao, Liu Yingfan, Peng Yanguo, Wang Manlin, WANG Meng, Ying Zuobin, Zhang Xiaoyu, Zhu Yixiao, etc.

My research interest is high-dimensional indexing and approximate query processings, including k nearest neighbor (kNN) search, k furthest neighbor (kFN) search, etc. Though at the initial stage of academia, I maintain a keen curiosity in exploring enchanted algorithms/ideas and a strong passion for technical practice.

WhatIs

  • 笔：一种受控制在介质上留下持久的可视痕迹的工具

    举例：比较直观的笔，如毛笔，铅笔，粉笔。都是由人用手控制，在纸或者黑板（介质）上留下可视痕迹，而且痕迹持久。 延伸出来，投影仪也是一种笔。由人控制（程序），在幕布（介质）上留下可视痕迹，而且持久。 再比如，烽火狼烟也是一种笔。由士兵控制（点燃干柴、湿柴、油脂等），在天空（介质）中留下可视痕迹（烟），而且要持久（让远方的人能够注意到）。 手机上的输入法也是一种笔，用户点击屏幕上的指定区域（操控），手机在另一个区域出现可视痕迹（点亮屏幕像素分布），而且持久。 所以，笔是一种非常基本的工具。
